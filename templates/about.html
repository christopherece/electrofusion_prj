Fusion Solutions Company Profile

Fusion Solutions are dedicated to providing you with the very best in polyethylene pipe welding products & services.

Our welding technicians are considered specialist in the pipe welding process due to our extensive daily involvement with some of Christchurchs leading civil construction companies, working on major infrastructure projects following the Christchurch earthquakes in 2011.

Fusion Solutions have undertaken a range of challenging and difficult repair work involving leaking pressure pipes and instigating a quality, proven solutions which result in major cost savings for our clients.

Fusion Solutions has also been involved in the horizontal infrastructure of numerous new and developing estates and sub divisions throughout Christchurch and Canterbury.

Specialist In Polyethylene Pipe Welding

Our ability to plan methodologies and design, develop & implement solutions for time critical projects is second to none, we stand by the quality and reliability of our butt fusion welding and polyethylene pipe joins.
