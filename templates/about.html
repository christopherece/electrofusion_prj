Fusion Solutions Company Profile

Pipeline Services was started after lengthy discussions within Huapai Plastic Welding management Slowly we have been able to evolve into Pipeline Services by adjusting our business to accommodate electrofusion welding. Our staff have completed the required training and qualifications for this position that they bring to PLS Our equipment is sourced from the UK and Australia and we pride ourselves to get the job done We currently offer pipe sizes 20mm-180mm but can offer larger pipe welding but this is per project needs We look forward to slowly growing in time

Specialist In Polyethylene Pipe Welding

Our ability to plan methodologies and design, develop & implement solutions for time critical projects is second to none, we stand by the quality and reliability of our butt fusion welding and polyethylene pipe joins.
